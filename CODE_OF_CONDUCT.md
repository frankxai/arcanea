# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Arcanea is built on the principle that creativity thrives in environments of mutual respect, intellectual honesty, and collaborative generosity. We are committed to providing a welcoming space for everyone regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Constructive, specific feedback focused on ideas and code
- Respect for differing perspectives and creative approaches
- Graceful acceptance of constructive criticism
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project
- Empathy toward other community members

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Personal attacks or inflammatory language
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Sustained disruption of discussions or contributions
- Any conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all Arcanea project spaces including GitHub repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, Discord, and any other community forums.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **conduct@arcanea.ai**. All reports will be reviewed promptly and confidentially. Project maintainers who do not follow or enforce the Code of Conduct may face temporary or permanent repercussions.
