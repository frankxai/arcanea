## FAQ for Media

**Q: How do you pronounce "Arcanea"?**
A: "ar-CANE-ee-uh" (like "arcane" + "ia")

**Q: Is Arcanea open source?**
A: The platform itself is proprietary, but we're planning to open-source specific components and release an API for developer integrations.

**Q: Does Arcanea create AI art/music/stories, or do users?**
A: Users create with AI companions. Arcanea provides the Guardians and Luminors, but the Essences belong to the creators. Think of it as collaborative creation, not automated generation.

**Q: What AI models power Arcanea?**
A: We integrate multiple leading AI models (GPT-4, Claude, Suno, etc.) through our orchestration layer, allowing Guardians and Luminors to use best-in-class AI for each creation type.

**Q: How do you handle copyright/ownership?**
A: Creators own their Essences. Remix culture operates under Creative Commons-style attribution with ARC value sharing. Commercial rights are available with Realm Builder tier.

**Q: Is this just for professionals or hobbyists?**
A: Both! Our free tier serves hobbyists and learners. Creator tier serves active creators. Realm Builder serves professionals and agencies.

**Q: How is this different from ChatGPT?**
A: ChatGPT is a conversational AI tool. Arcanea is a creative home where multiple AI types (including ChatGPT-style conversations) come together with unified identity, portfolio, teaching, and collaboration features.

**Q: Can educators use this in schools?**
A: Yes! We have an Academy Partner program for educators. Several schools are already using Arcanea for creative AI education.

**Q: What's the age requirement?**
A: 13+ with parental consent for minors. We're building age-appropriate content filters and educational safeguards.
