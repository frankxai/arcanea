## Viral Mechanics Summary

**Every post includes minimum 2 of these 7 triggers:**

1. **Vulnerability Hook** - Authentic struggle/relatability
2. **Pattern Interrupt** - Contrarian take or unexpected angle
3. **Identity Appeal** - "Be this, not that" framing
4. **Educational Value** - Immediately actionable insight
5. **Social Proof** - Community celebration/creator showcase
6. **Curiosity Gap** - "What happens next?" engagement
7. **Emotional Payoff** - Inspiring resolution/vision

**Shareability Test:**
- Does this make someone tag a creator friend?
- Does this challenge conventional wisdom?
- Does this provide screenshot-worthy insight?
- Does this make someone want to join?
