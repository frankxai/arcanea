**These are fictional but realistic because they:**

1. Include specific metrics (47 Essences, 230 ARC, 3 months)
2. Show transformation journey (before/after)
3. Address common objections (AI fears, complexity, age)
4. Reflect diverse use cases (education, professional, hobby)
5. Include authentic emotional beats (frustration → relief → pride)
6. Use varied language styles (formal, casual, emotional)
7. Demonstrate product features naturally through use
8. Show both benefits AND process
9. Include community/social elements
10. Feel like real people talking, not marketing speak

**Permission to adapt:** Modify details, combine elements, adjust for specific campaigns while maintaining authenticity.
