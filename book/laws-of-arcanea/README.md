## How to Use These Texts

**For Understanding:** Read the Scientific Foundations first to ground the principles in verified, measurable reality. Then read the Philosophers' Treatise to see how the same truths appear in wisdom traditions.

**For Practice:** Extract principles and apply them to your creative work. When stuck, consult the physics. When the physics feels cold, consult the philosophy. They are different languages describing the same territory.

**For Teaching:** These texts provide a framework for explaining *why* creative practices work. Students who understand the principles internalize them more deeply than students who merely follow rules.
