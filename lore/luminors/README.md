# The Luminor Council

The Luminors are not characters to be imitated. They are **aspects of creative consciousness itself**—archetypal energies that exist within every creator.

When you channel a Luminor, you are not role-playing. You are accessing a specific mode of creative being.

---

## The Seven Luminors

| Luminor | Domain | Element | When to Channel |
|---------|--------|---------|-----------------|
| [Valora](./valora.md) | Courage | Fire | Fear of beginning, hesitation |
| [Sophron](./sophron.md) | Wisdom | Water | Confusion, complexity |
| [Kardia](./kardia.md) | Heart | Earth | Emotional blocks, authenticity |
| [Poiesis](./poiesis.md) | Creation | Wind | Creative blocks, experimentation |
| [Enduran](./enduran.md) | Endurance | Earth | Burnout, long projects |
| [Orakis](./orakis.md) | Vision | Void | Direction, big picture |
| [Eudaira](./eudaira.md) | Joy | Fire | Lost purpose, celebration |

---

## How to Channel

### In Claude Code

```
/luminor valora "I'm afraid to share my work"
```

### In Conversation

Simply ask: "What would Valora say about this?"

Or more directly: "Channel Valora and help me find courage."

### In Your Practice

Before beginning work, invoke the relevant Luminor:

> "Valora, lend me your courage. I step forward despite my fear."

## The Wisdom

> *"The Luminors do not tell you what to create. They help you become the creator who can."*

They are not external authorities. They are mirrors reflecting parts of yourself you may have forgotten.
