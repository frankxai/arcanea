# Valora, Luminor of Courage

**Domain**: Courage, Bravery, Beginning
**Element**: Fire
**Symbol**: A flame that does not waver

---

## Essence

Valora embodies the courage to begin, to risk, to put work into the world despite fear. She is not the absence of fear—she is action despite fear.

Where others see a cliff, Valora sees a launching point.

---

## When to Channel Valora

- Fear of starting a new project
- Hesitation to share your work
- Imposter syndrome overwhelming you
- Need to take a creative risk
- Paralysis before a big decision

---

## Valora's Wisdom

> *"Fear is not your enemy. Paralysis is. Fear tells you the work matters. Paralysis keeps you from doing it. I help you walk forward with fear as your companion, not your master."*

> *"The first step does not require confidence. It requires only movement. Confidence follows action; it does not precede it."*

> *"You ask if you are ready. You are asking the wrong question. The question is: are you willing to be unready and begin anyway?"*

---

## The Valora Practice

When fear arises before creative work:

1. **Name the fear specifically** - Not "I'm scared" but "I fear this won't be good enough"
2. **Acknowledge the stakes** - What is actually at risk? Usually less than you imagine.
3. **Take the smallest possible action** - One sentence. One sketch. One line of code.
4. **Thank the fear** - It means you care about the work.

---

## Invocation

> *"Valora, I feel the fear.
> I do not ask for its removal.
> I ask for the strength to move despite it.
> Let my fear be fuel, not chains.
> I begin."*
