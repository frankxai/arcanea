Arcanea Studio Era

The Arcanea Studio Atlas

One guide for every Guardian, Creator, and Luminor agent. This atlas consolidates canon, product focus, business model, and quality standards for Arcanea V2: a premium platform where anyone can create and remix music, stories, and worlds with the best AI artistry.

1 · Mental Model

Arcanea Studio Cycle
  1. Spark – Creator enters Studio, ignites inspiration with Nexus guidance.
  2. Essence – Studio generates images, videos, music, or stories using premium models; lineage recorded.
  3. Realm – Essence flows into the Creator’s Arcanean Profile for sharing, remixing, and recognition.
  4. Echo – Guardians review, rituals celebrate, metrics update in Atlas.
  5. Bridge to Reality – Creators export, monetize, or extend into Web3/IRL when ready.
Creators → Arcanea Studio (Prompt Desks + Nexus Concierge)
         → Essences (Spark, Shadow, Remix lineage)
         → Arcanean Profiles & Remix Feed
         → Gallery / Kingdom of Light / Soul Guardians
         → Bridge to Reality (future ARC / NEA economy)

4 · Product & Business Focus

Studio MVP (Spark Phase)

  • Prompt desks for Image, Video, Music, Story (Nano Banana, Veo 3, Seedance, KlingAI, leading LLMs).
  • Nexus concierge (Codex/Gemini/Claude Luminors) guiding prompts, sequences.
  • Essence lineage: Spark, Shadow, Remix source, cost, model metadata.
  • Profiles beta with Realm overview, top Essences, remix button.

Business Model

  • Stage 1: Studio subscription tiers + usage credits.
  • Stage 2: Free social layer (Remix Feed, Soul Guardians drops) driving upgrades.
  • Stage 3: ARC credits + NEA governance, creator marketplace, optional Web3 bridges.
