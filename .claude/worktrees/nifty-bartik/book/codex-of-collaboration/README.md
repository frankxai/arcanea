## The Unity Gate Teaching

Collaboration is the domain of the Unity Gate (963 Hz), guarded by Ino and her Godbeast Kyuro, the Tiger-Dragon. Their teaching: separate beings can merge consciousness temporarily, working as one while remaining two.

- **Fire** — The passion that drives shared vision
- **Water** — The emotional flow between collaborators
- **Earth** — The grounded agreements and structures
- **Wind** — The free exchange of ideas
- **Void/Spirit** — The mystery of the collective mind, the consciousness that emerges between
