## Overview

The Book of Shadows is not for the beginning. It is for the descent—the Arc's Dissolution phase, where what was must return to potential before it can become what will be.

When creation fails. When meaning dissolves. When identity cracks. This book meets you in the darkness and offers companionship rather than false light.

**A Critical Distinction:**
The darkness of this book is not Shadow (corrupted Void). It is Nero's true darkness—the fertile unknown, the space of potential, the womb of transformation. Shadow destroys; Nero's darkness creates. The Book of Shadows teaches you to tell the difference.

## The Arc in Darkness

The dark night follows the Arc:
- **Dissolution** — What was falls apart (this is not failure; it is the cycle)
- **Return to Void** — Emptiness, but fertile with potential
- **Spirit's Whisper** — Consciousness discovers new truth in darkness
- **Evolved Potential** — You emerge changed, carrying new wisdom

Nero and Lumina are partners. The darkness you enter is not enemy territory—it is the other half of creation.
