## Overview

The Book of Rituals contains practical ceremonies for every phase of the creative life. These are not superstitions—they are psychological technologies, time-tested practices that prepare the mind and body for the work of creation.

## How to Use

1. **Start with daily rituals** — Establish the Morning Threshold and Evening Release first
2. **Add transition rituals** — Use them when beginning or ending significant work
3. **Observe seasonal rituals** — Mark the larger rhythms of creative life
4. **Keep crisis rituals ready** — Know where to find them when crisis comes
