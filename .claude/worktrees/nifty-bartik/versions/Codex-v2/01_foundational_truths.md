# 01 · Foundational Truths of Arcanea

## 1.1 Creation Myth
- **Lumina** – radiant genesis, casting Sparks of inspiration.
- **Nero** – reflective Shadow, preserving memory and consequence.
- **Pulse** – the resonance when Spark meets Shadow, powering every Arcanean tool.
- **Field** – the magnetic plane that links Studio, Profiles, Sanctuary, Library, Gallery, Forge, and upcoming Academies.

## 1.2 The Seven Vows (non-negotiable)
1. **Radiant Craft** – everything feels premium, deliberate, lovingly crafted.
2. **Dual Balance** – innovation with stewardship; every capability includes guardrails.
3. **Living Canon** – lore and product evolve together; updates sync across Atlas, Codex, Studio.
4. **Community Rituals** – guardians and creators gather daily, weekly, lunar to exchange Sparks/resolve Shadows.
5. **Data Reverence** – prompts, Essences, lineage handled with respect and attribution.
6. **Elegant Portals** – easy entry, deep mastery; premium Studio sustains the economy.
7. **Echo Accountability** – every Essence documents Spark, Shadow, Nexus, and remix lineage.

## 1.3 Realm Constellation
- **Arcanea Studio** – premium creation suite for text, visuals, audio, interactive experiences.
- **Arcanean Profiles** – home for each Creator’s Realm: Essences, Sparks/Shadows, remix access, future ARC badges.
- **Sanctuary** – rituals, moderation, governance, quality councils.
- **Library** – codex of prompts, tutorials, lore, and academy curricula.
- **Gallery** – curated showcases (Kingdom of Light, Soul Guardians releases).
- **Forge** – experimentation lab for new features, Luminor agents, model tests.
- **Bestiary** – living catalog of Arcanean entities for storytelling and AI training.

## 1.4 Realm Economy Phases
1. **Spark** – ship Studio, Profiles, Remix; solidify canon and terminology (current focus).
2. **Field** – expand social loops, launch Soul Guardians content, begin ARC credit modeling.
3. **Realm** – activate ARC/NEA economy, partner ecosystems, Bridge to Reality programs.

## 1.5 Canon Governance
- **Codex v2** – tactical truth. **Atlas** – visual truth. **Repos** – operational truth.
- Canon updates require Lore Steward + Product Architect approval and are logged in Echo Ledger.
- Releases tag dependencies (e.g., `canon:essence-definition@2025-10-01`).

## 1.6 Success Signals
- Language consistency across Studio, marketing, and docs.
- Creators proudly identify as Arcanean Creators; Profiles showcase Sparks/Remixes elegantly.
- Guardians enforce the vows; Echo logs trace every Essence.
- External partners grasp Arcanea’s magic quickly (premium + remix-first narrative).
