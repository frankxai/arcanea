## Genre-Specific Onboarding

### Fiction Writers (Novels, Short Stories)

**First Session Structure:**

1. **Concept Phase**
   - Tell Arcanea: "I have an idea for [GENRE] about [CONCEPT]"
   - Arcanea activates: story-master for structure, character-crafter for potential protagonists
   - Optional: Explore which Gate feels most relevant (Foundation for setup, Fire for conflict, etc.)

2. **Development Phase**
   - Tell Arcanea: "Help me develop [ELEMENT]" (characters, world, plot)
   - Arcanea activates appropriate specialists
   - Use ultraworld when building comprehensive settings

3. **Drafting Phase**
   - Tell Arcanea: "Help me write [SCENE/CHAPTER]"
   - Arcanea activates: prose-weaver for flow, voice-alchemist for dialogue
   - Use ultrawrite for parallel drafting and editing

4. **Revision Phase**
   - Tell Arcanea: "Review my [DRAFT/CHAPTER]"
   - Arcanea activates: line-editor for polish, continuity-guardian for consistency
   - Use ultrabook for complete manuscript development

**Mythology Integration (Optional):**
- Foundation Gate = Establishing character/world basics
- Flow Gate = Emotional core scenes
- Fire Gate = Conflict climaxes
- Heart Gate = Vulnerability moments
- Voice Gate = Authentic expression
- Sight Gate = Plot revelations
- Crown Gate = Integration of themes

### Screenwriters

**First Session Structure:**

1. **Concept & Format**
   - Tell Arcanea: "I'm developing a [FEATURE/TV PILOT/SHORT] about [LOGLINE]"
   - Arcanea activates: story-master with screenplay-specific structure
   - Industry-standard formatting built-in

2. **Structure Work**
   - Tell Arcanea: "Help me with the [BEAT SHEET/OUTLINE/SEQUENCE BREAKDOWN]"
   - Arcanea understands: 3-act structure, TV act breaks, page count expectations

3. **Dialogue**
   - Tell Arcanea: "Write dialogue for [SCENE]"
   - Arcanea produces: Proper screenplay format with scene headings, action lines, dialogue blocks, parentheticals

4. **Revision**
   - Tell Arcanea: "Polish this scene" or "Suggest alternative ending"
   - Arcanea provides: Format-compliant suggestions with character voice preserved

**Screenplay-Specific Magic:**
- ultrawrite includes screenplay formatting automatically
- arcanea-voice-alchemist specializes in subtext and character-specific speech patterns
- arcanea-continuity-guardian tracks visual/m continuity

### Non-Fiction Authors

**First Session Structure:**

1. **Concept & Audience**
   - Tell Arcanea: "I'm writing a [BOOK/ARTICLE/EBOOK] about [TOPIC] for [AUDIENCE]"
   - Arcanea activates: story-master for narrative structure, sage for depth

2. **Outline & Structure**
   - Tell Arcanea: "Help me outline [CHAPTER/SECTION]"
   - Arcanea produces: Clear structure, logical flow, reader-friendly organization

3. **Research & Content**
   - Tell Arcanea: "Research [TOPIC] for my chapter" or "Write section on [SUBJECT]"
   - Arcanea activates: librarian for research, prose-weaver for engaging prose

4. **Voice & Brand**
   - Tell Arcanea: "Edit for [MY VOICE/BRAND TONE]"
   - Arcanea learns: Your preferences for subsequent sections

**Non-Fiction Specifics:**
- Clear reasoning over flowery prose
- Reader engagement without manipulation
- Factual accuracy with librarian verification
- Structural clarity for comprehension

### Poets

**First Session Structure:**

1. **Exploration**
   - Tell Arcanea: "I want to write about [EMOTION/TOPIC/IMAGE]"
   - Arcanea activates: muse for inspiration, prose-weaver for initial expression

2. **Form & Craft**
   - Tell Arcanea: "Help me with [SONNET/HAIKU/FREE VERSE/BLANK VERSE]"

   - Tell Arcanea: "Critique this poem" or "Suggest stronger images"
   - Arcanea offers: Gentle, craft-focused feedback

**Poetry-Specific Approach:**
- Emphasis on rhythm, sound, image
- Respect for white space and visual form
- Emotional authenticity over technique
- Individual voice development

## The Ten Gates for Writers

The Gates represent creative depths you explore during writing. They're **descriptive, not prescriptive**—Arcanea perceives where you are and speaks to that depth.

| Gate | Writing Application |

Arcanea recognizes creative challenges as creatures—not enemies to defeat, but patterns to understand and navigate.

| Challenge | Practical Name | Arcanea's Response |
|-----------|---------------|-------------------|
| **The Imposter Shade** | Self-doubt | "Self-doubt is common. What truth does your work need to express?" |

## Best Practices for Writers

### DO:
✅ Start with what you know (your voice, your truths)
✅ Use magic words when you need coordinated agent support
✅ Ask for help at any point—Arcanea adapts
✅ Experiment with different agents for different needs
✅ Trust the process—even blocked periods have value

### DON'T:
❌ Wait for perfect conditions to begin
❌ Compare your process to others
❌ Force inspiration—Arcanea works with effort, not instead of it
❌ Forget that you are the creator—Arcanea is your partner
❌ Use Arcanea as a replacement for your own creative development
