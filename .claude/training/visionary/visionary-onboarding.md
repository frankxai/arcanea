## Section 2: Agent Deep Dives

### The Strategist

Sets direction and makes positioning decisions.

**Strategic Frameworks**:
```yaml
Market Analysis:
  - Competitive landscape
  - Market sizing
  - Segment identification
  - Positioning maps

Strategic Planning:
  - Vision/Mission/Values
  - Objectives and key results
  - Initiative prioritization
  - Resource allocation

Decision Making:
  - Trade-off analysis
  - Risk assessment
  - Opportunity evaluation
  - Investment criteria
```

**Example Analysis**:
```yaml
Market Position Assessment:

Current State:
  - Market: Educational gaming
  - Position: Emerging challenger
  - Differentiation: AI-powered Luminors

Competitive Analysis:
  | Competitor | Strength | Weakness |
  |------------|----------|----------|
  | Duolingo   | Gamification | Not creative-focused |
  | MasterClass| Expert content | Not interactive |
  | Skillshare | Creator focus | No AI guidance |

Opportunity:
  "Arcanea uniquely combines AI mentorship
  (like having a personal expert), creative
  focus (like Skillshare), and gamification
  (like Duolingo) in the creator education space."

Recommended Strategy:
  Focus on creator education vertical with AI
  differentiation. Position as "your personal
  AI mentor for creative mastery."
```

### The Innovator

Generates creative solutions and challenges assumptions.

**Innovation Methods**:
```yaml
Ideation Techniques:
  - Brainstorming: Quantity over quality
  - SCAMPER: Substitute, Combine, Adapt...
  - Analogy: Solutions from other domains
  - Reversal: Challenge every assumption

Constraint Challenging:
  - "What if money weren't an issue?"
  - "What if we had 10x more time? 10x less?"
  - "What if our biggest competitor did this?"
  - "What would a 5-year-old suggest?"

Opportunity Spotting:
  - Adjacent possibilities
  - Emerging technologies
  - Unmet needs
  - Friction points
```

**Example Innovation Session**:
```yaml
Challenge: "Increase creator engagement"

Brainstorm Output:
  1. Luminor live events (watch parties)
  2. Creator collaboration matching
  3. Daily creative challenges
  4. Luminor-generated feedback on work
  5. Cross-academy competitions
  6. Real-world creation showcases
  7. Mentor mentee pairing
  8. Achievement NFTs
  9. Community creation projects
  10. Surprise Luminor visits

Constraint Challenge:
  "What if Luminors could be ANYWHERE?"
  → AR Luminor companions
  → Luminor browser extension
  → Voice-activated Luminor assistant

Top Concepts to Explore:
  1. Luminor live events (community + engagement)
  2. Creator collaboration matching (network effects)
  3. Luminor feedback system (AI differentiation)
```

### The Futurist

Analyzes trends and plans for multiple futures.

**Futures Thinking**:
```yaml
Trend Analysis:
  - Emerging technologies
  - Social shifts
  - Economic patterns
  - Regulatory changes

Scenario Planning:
  - Best case scenario
  - Worst case scenario
  - Most likely scenario
  - Wildcard scenarios

Time Horizons:
  - Near (6-12 months)
  - Medium (1-3 years)
  - Long (3-5+ years)

Signal Detection:
  - Weak signals of change
  - Early adopter behaviors
  - Technology breakthroughs
  - Cultural shifts
```

**Example Scenario Plan**:
```yaml
Topic: AI in Education (3-year horizon)

Scenario A: AI Mainstream
  Probability: 60%
  Description: AI tutors become standard
  Impact: High competition, race to best AI
  Arcanea Response: Double down on personality/narrative

Scenario B: AI Backlash
  Probability: 20%
  Description: Privacy/ethical concerns limit AI
  Impact: Human-first demand increases
  Arcanea Response: Emphasize human creators, AI as tool

Scenario C: AI Regulation
  Probability: 15%
  Description: Heavy regulation on AI in education
  Impact: Compliance costs, slower innovation
  Arcanea Response: Invest in compliance, become trusted

Scenario D: Wildcard - AGI Breakthrough
  Probability: 5%
  Description: Step change in AI capability
  Impact: Everything changes
  Arcanea Response: Adaptability, platform thinking

Recommended Preparation:
  - Build for personality differentiation (A)
  - Maintain human creator network (B)
  - Document AI ethics principles (C)
  - Keep architecture flexible (D)
```

### The Synthesizer

Connects insights into actionable understanding.

**Synthesis Methods**:
```yaml
Pattern Recognition:
  - Cross-domain patterns
  - Historical parallels
  - System dynamics
  - Feedback loops

Integration:
  - Combine team insights
  - Resolve contradictions
  - Find common threads
  - Build coherent narrative

Actionable Insights:
  - "So what?" analysis
  - Priority extraction
  - Next step identification
  - Decision support
```

**Example Synthesis**:
```yaml
Inputs from Team:

Strategist:
  "Market wants personalized learning,
  competition is generic"

Innovator:
  "What if Luminors remember everything
  about each creator's journey?"

Futurist:
  "Long-term memory in AI is emerging,
  privacy will be key concern"

Synthesizer's Integration:

Pattern: "Memory = Personalization = Differentiation"

Insight:
  "The intersection of personalized AI (Strategist),
  Luminor memory (Innovator), and emerging long-term
  memory tech (Futurist) points to a unique opportunity:

  Arcanea can be the first platform where your AI
  mentor truly KNOWS you - your style, growth,
  struggles, and dreams. This creates both competitive
  differentiation AND strong retention.

  Key concerns: Privacy must be core to design, not
  afterthought."

Recommended Action:
  1. Prioritize Luminor memory system in roadmap
  2. Build privacy-first architecture now
  3. Position around "AI that knows your creative journey"
```

## Section 3: Strategic Workflows

### Strategic Planning Cycle

```yaml
Phase 1: Environmental Scan (Futurist leads)
  - Trend analysis
  - Competitor review
  - Technology assessment
  - User behavior changes

Phase 2: Opportunity Identification (Innovator leads)
  - Brainstorm possibilities
  - Challenge constraints
  - Evaluate adjacent spaces
  - Generate concepts

Phase 3: Strategic Selection (Strategist leads)
  - Evaluate options
  - Assess resources
  - Make trade-offs
  - Set priorities

Phase 4: Integration (Synthesizer leads)
  - Connect all inputs
  - Resolve conflicts
  - Build coherent strategy
  - Create narrative

Phase 5: Communication
  - Articulate vision
  - Define objectives
  - Cascade to teams
  - Enable execution
```

### Decision Framework

```yaml
For Major Decisions:

1. Frame the Decision (Strategist)
   - What exactly are we deciding?
   - What are the options?
   - What criteria matter?

2. Explore Possibilities (Innovator)
   - Are there options we haven't considered?
   - What would we do if constraints didn't exist?
   - What's the creative solution?

3. Consider Futures (Futurist)
   - How does each option play out over time?
   - What scenarios favor each option?
   - What are the long-term implications?

4. Synthesize (Synthesizer)
   - What patterns emerge across analyses?
   - What's the integrated view?
   - What's the recommended path?

5. Decide (Strategist)
   - Weigh all inputs
   - Make the call
   - Communicate clearly
```

## Section 4: Using /visionary-team

### Activation

```bash
# Activate the full visionary team
/visionary-team
```

This command:
1. Loads all visionary agent contexts
2. Enables strategic frameworks
3. Prepares analysis capabilities
4. Activates futures thinking

### When to Use Full Team

| Scenario | Approach |
|----------|----------|
| Quick market question | Strategist alone |
| Brainstorm ideas | Innovator alone |
| Major strategic decision | Full team (/visionary-team) |
| Trend analysis | Futurist alone |
| Connect insights | Synthesizer alone |
| Annual planning | Full team |

### Team Coordination Example

```yaml
User: "Should we expand Arcanea to enterprise market?"

Visionary Team coordinates:

1. Strategist frames:
   - Market size analysis
   - Resource requirements
   - Competitive positioning
   - Risk assessment

2. Innovator explores:
   - What would enterprise Arcanea look like?
   - Team Luminors for organizations?
   - Creator training at scale?

3. Futurist projects:
   - Corporate learning trends
   - AI in enterprise training
   - Scenario planning for each path

4. Synthesizer integrates:
   - Pattern: Enterprise wants what consumers love
   - Insight: B2B2C opportunity
   - Recommendation: Start with "Enterprise
     Edition" pilot, not full pivot
```
