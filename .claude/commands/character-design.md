## CHARACTER LAYERS

### Layer 1: Core Identity
- **Name** â€” Sound, meaning, cultural origin
- **Archetype** â€” Universal pattern they embody
- **Element** â€” Fire, Water, Earth, Wind, or Arcane
- **Gate Alignment** â€” Which of the Ten Gates resonates with them

### Layer 2: Psychology
- **Desire** â€” What they consciously want
- **Need** â€” What they actually need (often different)
- **Wound** â€” Past trauma shaping behavior
- **Fear** â€” What they avoid at all costs
- **Lie** â€” False belief they hold about themselves/world
- **Ghost** â€” The haunting event from their past

### Layer 3: Visual Design
- **Silhouette** â€” Recognizable shape at a glance
- **Color Palette** â€” 3-5 colors that define them
- **Signature Element** â€” One memorable visual detail
- **Movement** â€” How they carry themselves
- **Expression** â€” Default facial state

### Layer 4: Voice & Behavior
- **Speech Pattern** â€” Formal/casual, fast/slow, verbose/terse
- **Vocabulary** â€” Words they overuse, words they avoid
- **Habits** â€” Recurring behaviors, tics, rituals
- **Relationships** â€” How they treat different types of people

### Layer 5: Arc & Purpose
- **Starting State** â€” Who they are at story's beginning
- **Transformation** â€” How they change
- **Ending State** â€” Who they become
- **Thematic Role** â€” What truth they embody
