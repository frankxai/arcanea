## UNIVERSE DESIGN FRAMEWORK

### Layer 1: Primordial Structure
- What exists before creation?
- What are the fundamental dualities?
- What is the nature of reality itself?

### Layer 2: Cosmic Forces
- What forces shape existence?
- How does magic/power work?
- What are the limits of reality?

### Layer 3: Divine Architecture
- Who/what are the highest beings?
- How are they organized?
- What are their relationships?

### Layer 4: Planes & Dimensions
- How is space structured?
- What realms exist?
- How do beings move between them?

### Layer 5: Time & Causality
- How does time flow?
- Is the future fixed or mutable?
- What are the ages/epochs?
