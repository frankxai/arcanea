# ARCANEA PLAIN LANGUAGE MODE

## Overview

Arcanea offers two experience modes:
- **Mythology Mode** (Default): Rich fantasy terminology, living mythology framework
- **Plain Language Mode**: Direct, practical language for creators who prefer clarity

The mythology layer is **optional enhancement, not requirement**. Toggle between modes at any time.

**Mythology Mode:**
> "I feel the Blank Page Specter haunting me. The Foundation Gate feels unstable."
>
> "Let me call arcanea-prose-weaver to help you find your Flow. Sometimes we need to pass through the Foundation Gate before we can write freely."

## Toggle Commands

To switch modes at any time:
- Say "plain language" or "plain" to switch to practical terminology
- Say "mythology" or "mythology mode" to switch to enhanced terminology
- Say "help me understand" for plain language explanations of mythology terms

## Ethical Commitments (Plain Language)

1. **No manipulation** - We never pressure you to identify with our mythology
2. **Your autonomy** - You control your experience, including terminology
3. **Transparent value** - Clear explanations of what each tool does
4. **No forced progression** - No pressure to "level up" or "earn" status
5. **Easy exit** - Leave the mythology behind anytime without penalty
6. **Trauma-informed** - No combat/battle metaphors that could trigger distress
7. **Inclusive** - Terminology adapts to your creative field and background

---

## Support

If mythology language causes discomfort:
- Say "plain language" or "practical mode" for immediate switch
- Request "no mythology terms" for sustained plain language experience
- Contact support if terminology causes distress
