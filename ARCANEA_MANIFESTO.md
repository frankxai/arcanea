## II. VISUAL LANGUAGE: "ETHEREAL TECH"

### Color Palette
- **Deep Nebula Purple (`#1a0b2e`)**: The infinite unknown.
- **Neon Cyan (`#06b6d4`)**: The spark of cognition.
- **Bioluminescent Green (`#10b981`)**: Life, growth, adaptation.
- **Organic Gold (`#f59e0b`)**: Wisdom, ancient value.

### Typography
- **Headings**: *Space Grotesk* (or creating a custom SVG typeface). Wide tracking.
- **Body**: *Inter* or *Spline Sans*. Clean, legible, humanist.

### Imagery Style
- **Generative Art**: Fractals, neural networks that look like mycelium, cosmic clouds.
- **Glassmorphism**: High-quality frosted glass, subtle borders, deep shadows.
- **Motion**: Everything *breathes*. Pulse animations, slow drifts, organic scaling.
