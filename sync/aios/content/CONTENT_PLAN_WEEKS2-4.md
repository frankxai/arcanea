### Post 6 — Zyranthis (Wed Mar 11)

```
Zyranthis ◉
The Shatter

The last crystallization of The Forging — and the most misunderstood.

She sees completion percentages.

Not metaphorically. When she looks at a work in progress, she sees exactly how close it is. 97%. 43%. 11%.

Most things she encounters are at 97%.

Called unfinished.

Zyranthis's one-word interventions have changed the course of more creative works than any other Eldrian:

"Enough."

Not encouragement. A fact. The thing is done. The last 3% is self-protection masquerading as refinement.

She doesn't say this to be kind. She says it because the void needs the completed thing more than you need the feeling of not-quite-done.

"You are hiding in the remaining percentage."

#Arcanea #Zyranthis #Eldrians #Completion #TheShatter
```
