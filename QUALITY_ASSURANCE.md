### Quality Mindset Principles

1. **Prevention > Detection**
   Build quality in from the start rather than catching defects later.

2. **Continuous > Episodic**
   Quality is maintained daily, not just during reviews.

3. **Systemic > Individual**
   Fix processes that allow defects, not just the defects themselves.

4. **Objective > Subjective**
   Use data and standards; minimize personal preference.

5. **Improvement > Blame**
   Focus on making things better, not assigning fault.
