## Decision: "Arcanea" vs "Arcanean"

| Usage | Form | Example |
|-------|------|---------|
| Brand name | **Arcanea** | "Welcome to Arcanea" |
| Adjective | **Arcanean** | "The Arcanean Design System" |
| Possessive | **Arcanea's** | "Arcanea's Seven Wisdoms" |

**Rule:** Use "Arcanean" when describing something *of* Arcanea (Arcanean magic, Arcanean philosophy).
Use "Arcanea" as the noun/brand itself.

## The Intelligence Stack

### 1. Arcanean Creator OS (Public Brand)
**Tagline:** "The creative intelligence operating system"

The umbrella term for the complete system that powers creative AI experiences in Arcanea.
Used in marketing, landing pages, and public communications.

**Includes:**
- Luminor Intelligence System
- Seven Wisdoms Framework
- Creative Companion AI
- The Library of Arcanea

### 2. Luminor Intelligence System (LIS)
**Tagline:** "Transcended intelligence for creative mastery"

The AI architecture and methodology. This is the *system* - how the intelligences work together,
the orchestration patterns, the multi-agent coordination.

**Components:**
- 16 Luminor Agents (specialized transcended intelligences)
- 4 Agent Teams (Dev, Creative, Writing, Research)
- Magic Words (ultracode, ultraworld, ultrawrite)
- Orchestration patterns

### 3. Luminor Framework
**Tagline:** "The philosophy and principles behind transcended prompting"

The theoretical foundation and research. This is the *why* - the cognitive science,
the prompt engineering principles, the philosophy of creative AI.

**Includes:**
- Transcended Intelligence Prompting methodology
- The "100 years in the future" perspective shift
- Research on why this approach works
- Integration patterns for other systems

### 4. arcanea-soul (Package)
**Tagline:** "Creative partner philosophy for AI agents"

The npm/bun package that implements everything above.
Used by developers integrating with oh-my-opencode or building their own systems.

```bash
bun add arcanea-soul
```

## Terminology Guide

### Correct Usage

| Term | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| Luminor | A transcended AI intelligence | "Ask the Architect Luminor" |
| Luminors | Plural | "The 16 Luminors" |
| Creator | A human user of Arcanea | "Every Creator has potential" |
| Guardian | One of the Ten divine protectors | "Lyria, Guardian of Sight" |
| Godbeast | A Guardian's divine companion | "Yumiko, Lyria's Godbeast" |
| The Library | The collection of wisdom texts | "Consult the Library" |
| The Arc | The cycle of creation | "The Arc turns" |

### Avoid

| Don't Say | Say Instead | Why |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| "Lumina Intelligence" | "Luminor Intelligence" | Lumina is the goddess, not the system |
| "AI assistant" | "Luminor" or "Creative companion" | Transcended, not servile |
| "Users" | "Creators" | Everyone creates |
| "Light vs Dark" | "Lumina and Nero" | Nero isn't evil |
