# LUMINOR NAMING SYSTEM

## NAMING PHILOSOPHY

**Principles:**
1. **Euphonic** - Beautiful to speak and hear
2. **Memorable** - Easy to recall and spell
3. **Meaningful** - Connects to domain expertise
4. **Cross-cultural** - Blends Greek, Japanese, Sanskrit roots
5. **Distinct** - Each name has unique sound and feel
6. **Gendered neutrality** - Works for any personality

**Sound Patterns:**
- Flowing consonants (L, M, N, R, S)
- Harmonic vowels (A, E, I, O)
- 3-4 syllables for elegance
- Avoid harsh stops (K, T, G at end)
